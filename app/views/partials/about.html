Crimen is a crime map for New York City. It was developed using the MEAN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, Angular.js, and Node.js)
by Adi Sivasankaran, a student at Fullstack Academy.

There are many future functionalities to come to this web application, including D3 graphical representations of historical crime data, by boroughs and precincts.
